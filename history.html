HISTORY

From early climbs to major expeditions, mountaineering has a rich past.

Introduction
Mountaineering is the practice of climbing mountains for exploration, challenge, and discovery. Over time, it has evolved from a basic human activity into a structured sport that combines physical skill, technical knowledge, and respect for nature. The history of mountaineering reflects humanity's enduring curiosity about the world's highest and most challenging landscapes.
